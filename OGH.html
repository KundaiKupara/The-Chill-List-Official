Creepy Stories at Old Government House: A Twilight Tour of the Paranormal

Step into the shadows of history with a family-friendly ghost tour through the ground floor of Old Government House, a 222-year-old colonial mansion in Parramatta known for its eerie atmosphere and unexplained phenomena. This twilight experience is designed for young ghostbusters-in-training (ages 9–15) and their families, blending spooky storytelling with historical intrigue.

What to Expect

- Candlelit Walkthrough: Explore the house by faux candlelight, guided by experts who share spine-tingling tales of ghostly sightings and strange occurrences.
- Ghost Meters in Action: Learn how paranormal investigators use ghost meters to detect supernatural activity—and try them out yourself.



Haunted Hotspots

- The School Room, where the sound of a crying girl has been heard
- The Staircase, site of mysterious movements and unexplained noises
- A cupboard door that once opened on its own during a tour
- The Governor's Bedroom, where a modern NSW Governor reportedly saw a ghostly figure
- The Lady in Blue, a spectral presence seen drifting through the corridors
- Australia's First UFO Sighting?: Hear the tale of a strange event in Parramatta Park that some believe was a UFO encounter
Children are encouraged to wear their scariest costumes to enhance the spooky vibe, and the timing of the tour (4–5 PM) allows families to continue their evening with dinner in Parramatta afterward.
