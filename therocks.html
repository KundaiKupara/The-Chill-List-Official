The Rocks Markets: Sydney's Weekend Treasure Trove

Set against the backdrop of Sydney's historic harbourside precinct, The Rocks Markets are a beloved weekend ritual for locals and visitors alike. Every Saturday and Sunday from 10 AM to 5 PM, the cobblestone laneways of George Street, Playfair Street, and Jack Mundey Place come alive with a curated mix of creativity, culture, and culinary delights.

What You'll Find

- Handcrafted Goods: From artisan jewellery and ceramics to bespoke fashion and homewares, each stall showcases the work of passionate local makers.
- Art & Photography: Discover original artworks and prints that capture the spirit of Sydney and beyond.
- Natural Remedies & Beauty: Sample handmade skincare, essential oils, and wellness products crafted with care.
- Live Music: Talented buskers and performers add rhythm and soul to your shopping stroll.
- Street Food & Gourmet Treats: Indulge in international flavours, vegan bites, and decadent desserts—from sizzling dumplings to artisanal chocolates.

Why It's Special

Located just steps from Circular Quay, it's easy to reach by ferry, train, or bus.
The market reflects Sydney's diverse creative scene, offering a chance to meet the makers and hear the stories behind their craft.
It's not just a market—it's a cultural experience, blending history, art, and community spirit in one of the city's most iconic settings.
Whether you're hunting for a unique gift, tasting your way through global cuisines, or simply soaking up the atmosphere, The Rocks Markets are a must-visit destination that captures the essence of Sydney's creative heart.
